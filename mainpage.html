should i join?

We encourage everyone to join, whether you’re a ‘nerd’ or ‘geek’ like one of us, or someone who prefers to be more socially acceptable. That’s fine with us. Our members generally fit the following description, but like we said, we’re open to everyone;

We’re self-described geeks, we like to write code for computers. Oh yeah, math problems, those are fun. If we’re not found using a computer, than we’re generally found fixing one. We like science-fiction (two of us wrote our Junior Themes on science-fiction)
